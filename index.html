About

The NACE Luxurious is the top international hotel with the BEST services to provide. Our hotel contains exquisite rooms that contents each client's taste and satisfies each person's FLAVOR. Our mission is to make everyone's stay a comforting night to remember. Our rooms all have their own MAGIC to explore that was created by our team and their own personalities.

Elizabeth Wright

CEO & Founder

Founding the NACE Luxurious alomgside her bestfriends, she has always been in love with the feeling of Exquisite hotels. This idea brought her to open a luxury hotel with her childhood friends and have their own special rooms. Also, she is in love with the color black explaining the choice of her rooms.

Chloe Solis

VIP Investor and Founder

Being a co-founder of NACE and best friends with Elizabeth, She loved the idea of luxury and to be able to spend that elegance with her friend group. Fascinating over the pinks and greens, her style has a more earthy feeling while maintaining the elegance of a princess. Chloe is sweetest person you would meet.

Angeli Ventresca

VIP Architect and Founder

Being a fellow co-founder of NACE and best friends with Elizabeth, Angeli loves being elegant and maintaining a very clean way of living. Her style is like a magic 8 ball where one day it might be a Angelic white and the other day change to forest green. Angeli is one of the most gorgeous people you'll meet.

Nikki Rhodes

VIP Interior designer and founder

Being a co-founder of NACE and fellow best friends with Elizabeth, Nikki has a very calm and chill personality but expresses her sweetness to everyone. Nikki is the best person you will ever meet with the most comforting aura using the style of her pastel colors.
